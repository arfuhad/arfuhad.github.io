## Why Clean Architecture?

Clean Architecture separates your code into distinct layers, making it easier to test, maintain, and scale your Flutter applications.

### The Layers

1. **Presentation** – Widgets, BLoCs, and ViewModels
2. **Domain** – Entities, Use Cases, and Repository interfaces
3. **Data** – Repository implementations, Data Sources, and Models
